====== Platební model ======
Created Čtvrtek 26 květen 2016

**Jaký je nejrozšířenější platební model v oblasti cloudových služeb (platba za spotřebovaný výkon/paměť/přenos).**

* https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cloud_computing
* Cloud providers typically use a "**pay as you go**" model. This can lead to unexpectedly high charges if administrators do not adapt to the cloud pricing model [http://www.forbes.com/sites/centurylink/2014/02/27/wheres-the-rub-cloud-computings-hidden-costs/#7fc55d83b514]
* SaaS is sometimes referred to as "on-demand software" and is usually priced on a **pay-per-use** basis or using a subscription fee.
* IaaS example: https://www.digitalocean.com/pricing/
	* **monthly** vs **hourly**
